Our Company Story

SNS Fabrics Ltd. is working as a buying house in the field of garments industry. Our major concern is to play our role as a buying house for our customers. One of our primary responsibilities as a Buying House is to assure that the product purchased and released for shipment conforms to be Customer’s requirement. Our services include all that a buyer needs for successful buying. SNS Fashion Ltd has 2 years of experience on its back in dealing with all categories of garments. Our objective is to provide total customer satisfaction by offering a range of quality garments at our reasonable prices. Our swift response to our customers’ needs and well-maintained production schedules have earned us reputed customers in Germany, Canada, New Zealand, USA, UK, Australia, India. We also welcome customers from other parts of the world. “WE ARE READY TO TAKE CHALLENGE TO MEET UP THE DEMAND FOR ALL SORT OF HIGH-QUALITY GARMENTS AS PER BUYER REQUIREMENTS”

We provide unique services to the foreign buyers in procuring products of their choice and liking, Fashion Design, Model Industries, Apparel garments. Dealing with all types of garments product such as T-Shirts, Polo Shirts, Jeans, Trousers and Jackets in different colors and quality. Infinity Apparel Source Limited is going to start a chain store in worldwide and wishes to establish their own brand name throughout their good quality of product and services. More than 200 factories are interconnecting with us for our good reputation and well concern. We have been in the business for a long time to understand the unique and specific needs of the apparel industry
